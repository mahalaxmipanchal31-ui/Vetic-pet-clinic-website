About Vetic Pet Clinic

Vetic Pet Clinic is a 24x7 pet hospital located in the heart of Kalyan Nagar, Bengaluru. We provide expert veterinary care with compassion and dedication. Our experienced team strives to ensure the health, happiness, and well-being of your pets by offering comprehensive services tailored to their needs.

Our Services

  • 24x7 Emergency Veterinary Care
  • General Health Checkups
  • Vaccinations and Preventive Care
  • Pet Surgery and Treatments
  • Pet Nutrition and Wellness Advice
  • Grooming and Hygiene Services
  • Dental Care
  • Parasite Control and Flea Treatment
